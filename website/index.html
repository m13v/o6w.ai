What it does

Everything runs
on your machine.

No cloud servers between you and your AI. Full control, full privacy, full power.

Runs Locally

Your machine, your data. macOS or Windows. Works with Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, or fully local models via Ollama.

Any Chat App

WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, Signal, or iMessage. Your AI lives where you already chat. DMs and groups.

Persistent Memory

Remembers your preferences, context, and history. Becomes uniquely yours over time with local vector search.

Browser Control

Browse the web, fill forms, extract data from any site. Full Playwright-powered automation at your command.

System Access

Read and write files, run shell commands, execute scripts. Full access to your system, or sandboxed for safety.
